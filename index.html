Cats save the world

The world can feel loud, fast, and a little too serious. Cats remind us to slow down.
Quiet observers, expert nappers, and occasional agents of chaos — they turn ordinary moments into calm ones.

Maybe cats aren’t literally saving the world.
But they do make it softer.
